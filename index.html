Why you should join our club?

Summer Camps

Swimming camps are necessary for each competitive swimmer to achieve their goals, these swimming camps are sponsored and completely free for each member of our swimming club.

Nutrition

Nutrition is vital for each swimmer during the season and all of the copetitions that are ahead of them. Each member will receive three full meals, along with three protein bars.

Coaches

Our club has three swimming coaches, one head-coach and two assistant coaches. They all have level three coaching qualification, which is necessary when dealing with competitive and elite performers.

Equipment & Clothing

Our members receive free swimming equipment and the necessary clothing for all official competitions.
